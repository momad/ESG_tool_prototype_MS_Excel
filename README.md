# ESG_tool_prototype_MS_Excel
This is a prototype tool for ESG monitoring with a focus on meeting the sustainability data requirements of textile MSMEs exportign materials to high end fashion brands in the European Union.

The scope of the ESG tool prototype is limited to material topics and ESG issues that fall under the environment pillar of ESG. The tool offers a mechanism to measure, report and validate 73 environmental metrics across four material categories — Climate Change, Water and Marine Resources, Circular Economy and Waste Management, and Biodiversity along with a General Information category. 
These material topics fall within the scope of the environmental pillar of ESG.
Some of the GHG emission factors used in the tool prototype use deprecated values due to unavailability of India-specific emissions factors. 
Users should keep in mind that this is a tool prototype and offers a roadmap. It should not be treated as a ready to use product.
